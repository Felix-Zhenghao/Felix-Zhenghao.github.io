I went to Yuze Foundation for summer internship in 2022. There I worked with a group of enthusiastic people. In many remote areas of China, primary and secondary schools struggle to offer physical education, music, and other courses due to a lack of teachers, with many educators teaching three or four different subjects. Yuze Foundation has found a sustainable and extremely cost-effective solution to this problem that not only motivates local teachers to enhance their professional skills but also addresses the shortage of qualified teaching staff. Thousands of kids have blossomed with smiles during the first music, art, and physical education class in their life.
